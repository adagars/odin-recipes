Caprese Salad

Description

Savor the freshness of a classic Caprese salad that combines the vibrant flavors of tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil. This light and refreshing salad is perfect for summer gatherings or as a side dish to complement a variety of main courses. With its colorful presentation and simple preparation, it's sure to be a hit on any table.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • A bunch of fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the tomatoes into rounds, approximately 1/4-inch thick.
  2. Similarly, slice the fresh mozzarella cheese into rounds of the same thickness.
  3. Arrange alternating slices of tomatoes and mozzarella on a serving platter.
  4. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  5. Drizzle the salad with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Allow the flavors to meld together for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve the vibrant Caprese salad as a refreshing appetizer or a delightful side dish.
