Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is loved for its layers of rich flavors and comforting textures. To prepare a delicious lasagna, start by browning ground beef and Italian sausage in a large skillet. Once cooked, drain any excess grease and add diced onions, minced garlic, and chopped bell peppers for added depth of flavor. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of Italian herbs like oregano, basil, and thyme, allowing the sauce to simmer and develop its robust taste. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, and beaten eggs to create a creamy and cheesy filling. Then, it's time to assemble the lasagna. Layer the sauce, lasagna noodles, and cheese filling in a baking dish, repeating the process until all the ingredients are used. Top it off with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle some additional Parmesan for a golden, gooey crust. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the noodles are tender. Finally, let it rest for a few minutes before serving, allowing the flavors to meld together. This lasagna recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser, offering a comforting and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell peppers
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian herbs
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat. Drain any excess grease.
  3. Add diced onions, minced garlic, and chopped bell peppers to the skillet. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs (oregano, basil, thyme). Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, and beaten eggs to create the cheese filling.
  6. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature (usually around 375°F or 190°C).
  7. Assemble the lasagna by layering the ingredients in a baking dish: start with a layer of sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, and then a layer of the cheese filling. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used.
  8. Top the final layer with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle some additional Parmesan on top.
  9. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for approximately 30-40 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the lasagna to set and makes it easier to cut into portions.
  11. Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
